Our Story

From a small idea to a neighborhood warm spot — learn how we became your daily comfort.

How It Began

The Sai’s Café started with a simple belief: great coffee and good company make life better. Founded by two friends who loved coffee and conversation, we opened our doors to bring carefully roasted beans, lovingly baked pastries, and a comfortable place to connect.

We focus on local suppliers, seasonal menus, and relentless attention to detail — from the roast profile to the foam on your cappuccino. We want The Sai’s to be the living room for the neighborhood.
